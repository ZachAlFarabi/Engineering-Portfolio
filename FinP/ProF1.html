ART - Financial management System [Treasurer]



● Objectives

As the Treasurer and Financial Director of the Adelaide Rover Team, no existing methods of managing financial systems, procurement, and capital existed. Thus, the objective of my role became to create the financial infastructure and procurement pipeline. This also included running a pioneering finance and sponsorship team, which diversified skillsets within the team.

● Technical Details

  • Created journalising standards for cash, assets, and liabilities within the team. An infastructure was created in a dynamic database that linked to technical development. This was then used as an allocated role for a team member within my finance team.
  • Managing, budgeting, and overseer of all finance and cashflow operations for a AUD$35 000 rover system. Supported the movement and expansion of the team's workshop space with a largely growing inventory.
  • Contracting and managing business meetings with internal schools, creating a revenue of AUD$35 645 alone, ending with a balance 5x of the starting balance of the Research Account. Additionally, aided in the growth of the business account, ending in a balance AUD$2000 more of the starting balance.
  • Pioneered an experimental designated finance team and field to diversify skill sets within the team - later adopted for the 2025-2026 cycle. Adopted acting sponsorship director role within December and January. Additionally created policies and protocols to control standards within team management and legalities.
