Project F.01

ART - Financial management System [Treasurer]



● Objectives

As the Treasurer and Financial Director of the Adelaide Mechanical Engineering Student Society, there was a lack of financial management or budgetting and analysis methods. Thus, when I inheritted the role, finances were not in a good state. I developed easy to follow journalising and analysis systems for future treasurers of the club, and to help with operations. This helped stabilise finances for the future committee. Additionally, I helped with running events and media operations. within the team.

Go back!

● Technical Details

  • Managed all finance and cashflow operations, standardising consistent financial journalising for any outgoing or ingoing cashflows, including documentation.
  • Analysed financial trends of cashflows, designing an optimised budget for event and merchandise operations, now fully integrated for future committees. By doing this, I was also able to stabilise the club's finances, in which when I inheritted the role, the club was not in a good financial position.
  • Led management of AMESS biggest event, merchandise operations, partnering in other events and media activities.
