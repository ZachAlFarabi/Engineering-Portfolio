Project E.05

Cobalt Extraction from Asteroids using a Continuous Flow Reactor



● Objectives

Cobalt extraction from asteroids using a continuous flow reactor is a novel research, in which continuous research in space mining have been prevelant. Pockets of cobalt and iron have been found to be large in asteroids. This research aims to create a method to extract it.

Go back!

● Technical Details

  • Experimentally researched means of extracting cobalt, nickel, and iron inside of asteroids sample using a continuous flow reactor.
  • Extensive literature review and research on existing methods, and in-development methods known.
  • Presented and networked scientific progress to field experts collaborating with a small team within a short timeline.
